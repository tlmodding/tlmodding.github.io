##### Welcome to **Tomodachi Life Modding Wiki**!
This wiki is a comprehensive resource for anyone interested in modding **Tomodachi Life** games. Whether you're a beginner looking to **install your first mod** or an experienced modder wanting to **create your own**, you'll find everything you need here.

It is a community-driven open-source project, and we encourage contributions from anyone who wants to help improve it. If you have any suggestions or want to contribute, please feel free to reach out to us on our [Discord](https://discord.gg/YHFNTvXrdE) or open an pull request on our [GitHub Repository](https://github.com/tlmodding/tlmodding.github.io).

## FAQ 

<details>
<summary>Is there any risks on using mods?</summary>

When used correctly there is no risk on using mods, specially when played offline on a **EmuMMC** or with a emulator. 

</details>


<details>
<summary>Should I use emuMMC or sysMMC?</summary>

Since there is no real **online** fuctionalities on Tomodachi Life games, we highly recommend using EmuMMC (emunand), since it's a safe place to mod without having to worry about bans.

</details>
