**UGC** stands for **User Generated Content**, and it's a built-in feature of *Tomodachi Life: Living the Dream* that allows players to create their own custom content for the game, such as custom clothing, buildings, foods and even facepaints for their Miis.

## Clothes Templates

**Chloe** (`chloegoaty`) has created a set of **Clothes templates** to help you create your own UGC content for the game. You can download the templates using the button below.

<LinkCard
  title="Download Clothes Templates"
  href="/downloads/clothes_templates.zip"
  download
  description="Click here to download the Clothes templates"/>
